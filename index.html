What is dark energy?

Dark energy is the name given to the solution to the expansion of the universe.

According to Dr. Christopher Baird at wtamu.ed, dark energy is the force that pushes apart galaxies and making the universe expand. The increased rate of expansion should be impossible without an external force, and this external force is deemed dark energy.

What is dark matter?

Dark matter is a force that tries to pull the universe together, and make it collapse.

Dark matter is basically the exact opposite of dark energy. It tugs on galaxies and pulls on the universe. It attempts to bring down the universe and create the Big Crunch.

What is the difference?

In short, dark matter attracts, and dark energy repels.

According to Hetdex.org, the main difference between the two is that dark energy tries to make the universe collapse, and dark energy tries to make it expand.
